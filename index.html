Academic Research

Spatiotemporal Assessment of Ecological Quality Using RSEI

A Five-Year Study of Lahore and Hafizabad Districts, Punjab, Pakistan (2021–2025)

This project assessed ecological environmental quality in Lahore and Hafizabad Districts using the Remote Sensing Ecological Index (RSEI) framework. Multi-temporal Landsat 8 satellite imagery from 2021–2025 was analyzed to evaluate seasonal and long-term environmental changes. The study integrated vegetation, moisture, dryness, and temperature indicators through Principal Component Analysis (PCA) to monitor ecological conditions and compare urban and semi-urban environments for sustainable planning and environmental management.
